Welcome to Havant Hoops

A fun and friendly club for all abilities

Whether you're looking to improve your fitness, make new friends or challenge yourself on court look no further. We are a friendly inclusive club based in Havant offering something for everyone. We run back to netball weekly sessions, fitness sessions, we have 3 teams of varying abilities that play in a local league and we have regular social events.

Teams

Havant Hoops currently run 3 teams of varying abilities, the blue team, the yellow team and the green team, who all play in the local About Netball league. The blue team recently won division 3 and now play in division 2, the yellow and green teams both play in division 6. We are always looking for new players of all abilities to strengthen our squad so if you're looking to play in a fun local league come along to one of our back to netball sessions or drop us a message.

Coaches

Kerry W and Kerry B have been our coaches and captains since the beginning of 2020 (just before the world went into lockdown). They have been the strength and the spine of the team that have kept us together. Driving and pushing us to the best of our abilities. 2 promotions, including a Championship and stability in where we are now. Couldn’t ask for more!
